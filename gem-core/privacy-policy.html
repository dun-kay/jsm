2. What this policy covers

This policy covers information we collect or receive through Gem Core, our support process, and the service providers and SDKs used to operate, support, and promote the app.

3. Information we collect

We aim to keep data collection limited and practical.

3.1 Information used for app functionality and support

We may collect and store limited app-related information such as:

  • a unique in-app Support ID assigned to your installation or profile;
  • basic gameplay/account state, such as last level reached and related progress information;
  • purchase-related records needed to validate, restore, or support in-app purchases;
  • basic technical or diagnostic information needed to operate the app, investigate issues, and respond to support requests.

3.2 Support information

If you contact us through the support page, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your message, screenshots, device details, purchase details, or your Support ID.

3.3 Advertising and install attribution

We use third-party SDKs, including Meta and TikTok SDKs, to help measure and attribute app install advertising campaigns. These SDKs may collect device, campaign, and attribution-related information in accordance with their own policies and configurations.

For iOS, we currently intend to use Apple's privacy-preserving attribution framework, including SKAdNetwork, rather than requesting App Tracking Transparency permission for user-level tracking.

4. What we do not intend to collect

We do not intentionally collect more personal information than is reasonably needed to operate Gem Core, provide support, process and support purchases, measure install campaigns, and maintain app functionality.

We do not ask players to create a traditional account using a name, email address, or password in normal app use unless that changes in a future version.

5. How we use information

We use information to:

  • operate and maintain Gem Core;
  • save and restore essential gameplay or purchase-related state;
  • provide customer support;
  • detect, prevent, and investigate bugs, abuse, fraud, or misuse;
  • measure the effectiveness of install advertising campaigns;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

6. Legal basis and consent

Where applicable, we process information because it is necessary to provide the app and related services, to support our legitimate interests in operating and improving the app, to comply with legal obligations, or based on your consent where required by law.

7. Third-party services and SDKs

Gem Core may use third-party providers such as:

  • Supabase for first-party backend and app-support functionality;
  • Apple App Store / Google Play for distribution, billing, and purchase handling;
  • Meta SDK for install ad attribution and campaign measurement;
  • TikTok SDK for install ad attribution and campaign measurement.

These providers may handle information under their own terms and privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies where relevant.

8. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information:

  • with service providers who help us operate the app;
  • with app store and payment platform providers to process or support purchases;
  • with advertising and attribution partners to measure install campaigns;
  • where required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the app.

9. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for app functionality, purchase support, customer support, security, dispute resolution, legal compliance, and business record-keeping.

11. International data handling

Our service providers may store or process data in countries outside your own. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable law.

12. Security

14. Your rights
