Mr. Blond

Master SNAP, crush your opponents, and help Mr. Blond find his missing wife.

The real SNAP game they don't want you to know about. Welcome to the world of Mr. Blond — a chaotic, story-driven card game where quick reflexes, sharp memory, and SNAP instincts are the only way to survive.

Mrs. Blond has vanished. All she left behind was a cryptic message… and a trail of highly competitive card sharks. To bring her back, you’ll need to outplay them all — from ex-gang members to zen masters and SNAP junkies — one high-stakes game at a time.
